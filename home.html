Report Cyber Crime

How to report

  1. Stay calm and collect evidence: screenshots, URLs, emails, phone numbers.
  2. Do not delete chats or messages; note date, time, and handles.
  3. File a complaint on the National Cyber Crime Portal.
  4. For financial fraud, contact your bank immediately and then report.
